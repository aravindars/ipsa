Tournament Regulations

1. All participants must register by the given deadline and provide valid identification during registration.

2. The tournament will consist of singles and doubles events. Participants may enter only one event per category.

3. All players must wear appropriate badminton attire, including non-marking shoes. Any participant not adhering to the dress code will be disqualified.

4. The matches will be played in a knockout format, with the winners progressing to the next round. Each match will be best of 3 games, with each game played up to 21 points.

5. The official tournament referee will make the final decision on all disputes, including decisions regarding match conduct, scoring, and player eligibility.

6. Participants are required to check-in at least 30 minutes before the scheduled match time. Failure to do so will result in a forfeit of the match.

7. Any form of cheating, unsporting behavior, or disruptive conduct will lead to immediate disqualification from the tournament.

8. The tournament organizers reserve the right to modify the regulations at any time and will notify participants of any changes.

9. Participants must respect the facilities and ensure that all areas remain clean and orderly throughout the event.

10. All participants agree to abide by these regulations and acknowledge that their participation in the tournament is at their own risk.
