Who We Are

The primary goal of our association is to bridge the communities of Poland and India by offering badminton training to children of both nations. We aim to create a space where members of the Indian community in Poland can connect, socialize, and engage in physical activities.

Founded as Ace Badminton Club in 2023, we have evolved into the Indo-Polish Badminton Sport Association, a vibrant community centered around fitness and cultural exchange.

Youth Training

Professional coaching tailored for Polish and Indian children to develop skills and discipline.

Social Hub

Creating a home-away-from-home for the Indian diaspora to connect through active living.

Successfully transitioning from a local club to a recognized Sport Association in just 2 years.
